💡 Budgeting Tips for Students
50/30/20 Rule — Spend 50% on needs (food, travel), 30% on wants (entertainment), and save 20%.
Track Daily — Even small expenses add up fast. Log every purchase — that daily chai can be ₹600/month!
Plan Ahead — Know your exam seasons. Books and stationery cost more at semester start. Budget accordingly.
Avoid Impulse Buys — Wait 24 hours before any unplanned purchase over ₹500. You'll skip 80% of them.
